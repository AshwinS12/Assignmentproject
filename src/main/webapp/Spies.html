Spy family

Having successfully passed the first phase of Eden Academy's admissions process, the newly
created family must now go through a family interview to dupe Housemaster Henry Henderson and the faculty
of Eden Academy into thinking they are an upper-class family.
After Anya manages to enroll into Eden Academy, she is assigned to Cecile Hall, and must approach and befriend
the second son of Donovan Desmond, Damian Desmond. In the process, she befriends rich girl Becky Blackbell.
Ostania's State Security Service, the greatest enemy of WISE and Twilight, begins making waves near the family.
At the same time, Yor's younger brother Yuri
Briar finds out about her marriage and plans a surprise visit to their household.
To get a step closer to becoming Imperial Scholars, Anya and her fellow classmates
begin trying to earn Stella Stars through various activities.
During the East-West Summit, a group of terrorists from Berlint University
begin targeting Westalian Minister Brantz to disrupt the
summit and start a war between Westalis and Ostania. Agent Twilight is tasked to put a stop to their nefarious plans. Meanwhile
, Anya meets a dog with mysterious powers at an adoption fair.
As the midterm exams approach, Anya and her classmates have to face a total of four tests. Anya must avoid failing
her exams or face the threat of being several Tonitrus Bolts' closer to expulsion instead.
The assassin Thorn Princess receives a mission to protect Olka Gretcher and her son from a group of skilled assassins
aboard the Princess Lorelei cruise ship. However, Yor must also protect her secret identity from
being discovered by Anya and Loid, who also win tickets to the same cruise ship.
Many of WISE's spies and personnel must carry out their work in attempt to bring peace between Ostania and Westalis.
Among them is Twilight, who must reflect back on why it is he became a spy and how he'll proceed with his dark past behind him.
